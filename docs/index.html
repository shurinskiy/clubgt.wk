About us

The supercar owners club unites passed people. Our goal is to become your guide to the world of motorsport. We held supercar rallies, organize track days on racing tracks, have sunday brunches with interesting people, visit museums, exhibitions and sports plants. Club members are both professional pilots and amateurs. The club team is developing the racing diraction and can provide a full package of services and PR assistance in integration into the motor-sport environment.
